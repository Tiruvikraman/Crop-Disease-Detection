Crop Yield Prediction

Welcome to the Crop Yield Prediction tool! This page allows you to predict the agricultural yield for various crops in specific regions and years. Whether you're a farmer, researcher, or agricultural enthusiast, our predictive model can help you make informed decisions.


How to Use

  1. Crop:

    Choose the crop you want to predict the yield for from the dropdown menu. We support a wide range of crops, so find the one you're interested in.

  2. Select Season:

    Specify the season (e.g., Kharif, Rabi, Summer, Winter, Autumn) during which the crop is grown.

  3. Select State:

    Pick the state in India where you're interested in making the prediction. Different states have varying climatic conditions that can affect crop yield.

  4. Provide Additional Information:

    Depending on the crop, you may need to provide additional information such as the area cultivated, annual rainfall, fertilizer usage, and pesticide usage. These factors can significantly impact yield.

  5. Predict Yield:

    After entering all the necessary information, click the "Predict Yield" button. Our predictive model will analyze the data and provide you with an estimate of the crop yield for the specified year and location.
